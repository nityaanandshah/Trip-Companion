# TerraMates - Features Guide

## What is TerraMates?

TerraMates is a social platform that helps travelers find and connect with compatible travel mates for their adventures. Think of it as a way to discover travel companions who share your interests, budget, and travel style!

---

## For Travelers

### 1. Create Your Profile

- **Personal Information**: Add your name, bio, and age
- **Profile Picture**: Upload a profile photo to help others recognize you
- **Travel Preferences**: Share what kind of traveler you are

### 2. Discover Trips

- **Browse All Trips**: See trips posted by other travelers
- **Filter by Preferences**: Find trips that match your:
  - Destination
  - Travel dates
  - Budget range
  - Trip duration
- **Search Keywords**: Look for specific vibes or activities (e.g., "hiking", "beach", "culture")
- **Sort Options**: View trips by latest, soonest departure, or budget

### 3. Save Trips You Like

- **Bookmark Trips**: Save interesting trips to review later
- **Your Bookmarks Page**: Access all your saved trips in one place
- **Easy Management**: Add or remove bookmarks anytime

### 4. Request to Join Trips

- **Join Button**: Click "Request to Join" on trips you're interested in
- **Status Tracking**: See if your request is pending, approved, or declined
- **View Trip Members**: See who else is going on the trip
- **Profile Viewing**: Check out other travelers' profiles before joining

### 5. Notifications

- **Stay Updated**: Get notified when:
  - Someone requests to join your trip
  - Your join request is approved or declined
  - You receive new messages in trip chats
  - A trip you're interested in becomes full
- **Notification Center**: View all notifications in one place
- **Mark as Read**: Keep track of what you've already seen

### 6. Real-Time Chat

- **Trip Group Chats**: Chat with all trip members in real-time
- **Message History**: See all previous conversations
- **Online Indicators**: See who's currently online
- **Typing Indicators**: Know when someone is typing
- **Mobile Friendly**: Chat works seamlessly on mobile and desktop
- **Direct Access**: Open chat from trip pages or notifications

## For Trip Organizers

### 1. Create a Trip

Share your travel plans with essential details:

- **Destination**: Where you're going
- **Dates**: When you're traveling (or mark as tentative)
- **Budget**: Your estimated cost range
- **Group Size**: How many people you need
- **Description**: Tell others about your trip plans
- **Mood Board**: Upload up to 5 images to inspire others
- **Trip Vibe**: Describe the atmosphere you're looking for

### 2. Manage Trip Status

- **Draft**: Work on your trip privately before publishing
- **Open**: Accepting join requests
- **Tentative**: Dates aren't confirmed yet
- **Full**: Trip has reached capacity
- **Completed**: Trip is finished

### 3. Review Join Requests

- **View Requests**: See who wants to join your trip
- **Check Profiles**: Review requester profiles before deciding
- **Approve or Decline**: Accept compatible travelers
- **Manage Members**: View all trip attendees with their details
- **Remove Members**: Remove participants if needed

### 4. Edit Your Trips

- **Update Details**: Change any trip information
- **Modify Images**: Add or remove mood board photos
- **Adjust Capacity**: Change group size requirements
- **Delete Trips**: Remove trips you no longer need

### 5. Coordinate with Members

- **Group Chat**: Message all trip members at once
- **Share Updates**: Keep everyone informed about changes
- **Plan Together**: Discuss details with your travel group
- **Build Community**: Get to know your travel companions

## Common Use Cases

### Case 1: Solo Traveler Looking for Companions

1. Browse trips going to destinations you're interested in
2. Filter by budget and dates that work for you
3. Bookmark trips you like
4. Review the trip organizer's profile
5. Request to join compatible trips
6. Wait for approval
7. Chat with the group once approved
8. Coordinate details together

### Case 2: Planning Your Own Trip

1. Create a trip with all the details
2. Upload inspiring photos
3. Publish your trip as "Open"
4. Receive join requests from interested travelers
5. Review requester profiles
6. Approve compatible travelers
7. Use group chat to plan together
8. Update trip status as you coordinate

### Case 3: Finding Last-Minute Travel Buddies

1. Search for trips departing soon
2. Sort by "Soonest Departure"
3. Request to join immediately
4. Chat with organizer to confirm details
5. Join the adventure!

## Using the Platform

### Dashboard

Your central hub showing:

- **Quick Stats**: Trips created, trips joined, bookmarked trips
- **Recent Activity**: Your latest trips and bookmarks
- **Quick Actions**: Create trip, browse trips, view bookmarks
- **Notifications Badge**: Unread notification count

### My Trips Page

Manage all your trips in one place:

- **Trips I'm Organizing**: Trips you've created
- **Trips I'm Attending**: Trips you've been approved for
- **Pending Requests**: Trips you're waiting to hear back on
- **Rejected Requests**: Trips where your request was declined (removable)

### Bookmarks Page

- View all trips you've saved
- Quick access to interesting trips
- Easy bookmark management

### Notifications Page

- All your notifications in chronological order
- Quick actions (approve/reject directly from notifications)
- Mark individual or all notifications as read
- Delete notifications you don't need
